PSD File Layer Guide


PSD Files are well organized with layer name. All elements are 100% scalable vector graphics and you can free scale, distort, grow, reduce size and edit. To open layer panel in Photoshop, just click on "Window" menu at top bar and open "Layers" from there, or Short key is "F7", for mor details, check screen shot below.

You can easily view / manage / arrange all layer By clicking on Arow, you can open folder (group of layer) of that perticular section.

Read more about how to manage Groups and layer in photoshop: design.tutsplus.com
